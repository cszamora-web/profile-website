DEVELOPED A TOURIST SPOT WEBSITE


During our 1st-semester in our 2nd-year level, our instructor in major subject

gave us a project to create a website page of our chosen municipality in the province of Antique.

We first need to pick only 5 barangays in the municipality. Then our job is to provide the

basic and latest informations, like the current officials, emergency numbers, history of the barangay and some photos.

Last but the most important thing is to promote and show off the beauty of each barangay.

It's either the already known spots or the unknown.
